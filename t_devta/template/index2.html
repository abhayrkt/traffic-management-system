    • About us

    This Traffic Management System is primarily based on Intelligent Transportation System domain for improving traffic flow. Traffic Devta! is a top-down management perspective that integrates technology primarily to improve the flow of vehicle traffic and improve safety.
    Following are the primary goals :
    Increase transportation system efficiency,
    Enhance mobility,
    Improve safety,
    Reduce fuel consumption and environmental cost,
    Increase economic productivity.

    Several techniques exist to reduce delay of traffic. Generally the algorithms attempt to reduce delays (user time), stops, or some other measure of effectiveness.
